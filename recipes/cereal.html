The Bowl of cereal

One of many recipes brought to you by Brandon, AKA stopandthinkpad, AKA Rofth

Description

The Bowl of Cereal. Nothing, and I mean nothing, screams breakfast quite like cereal does. Not only does it scream breakfast but it is something that is just as good for lunch, and or (dare I say) dinner. with a reach from the poverty line up to the Elon's, billionares, The Bowl of Cereal stands and will continue to stand the test of time as one of the best meals for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, reaching into the depths of the snack category. To be eaten with or without milk, I cannot say anymore but, ENJOY.

Ingredients

  • Milk: 2% or whole milk work just fine. Try and avoid the dairy alternatives if possible and dietary restrictions permiting of course
  • Cereal: Frosted mini-wheats if a healthy(ish) recommended

Optional ingredients:

  • Fruit
  • Nuts

Steps:

  1. Pour cereal into a bowl.
  2. Pour milk into the bowl over the cereal: the amount varies depending on the cereal type and personal preference.
  3. NOT REQUIRED: Add fruit and or nuts as desired or leave plain.

Some have even swapped step 1 and 2 to adjust milk to cereal ratios properly.
