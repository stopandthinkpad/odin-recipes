The Hotdog

One of many recipes brought to you by Brandon, AKA stopandthinkpad, AKA Rofth

Description

The Hotdog (also known as the glizzy) is not only a hit in the world of Newyork street food, it is a classic in the world of the middle class. When it comes to holidays like the 4th of July, New Years, heck.. even a random HOT day in the summer, The Hotdog is a staple. This recipe is a simple one so no need to grab the measuring spoons or stress what is in your spice cabinet. We will also add in some options for those of you who want to get "fancy" and by "fancy" I mean going over some alternative ways to heat The Hotdog.

Ingredients

  • Hotdogs: any pre-packaged hotdogs will do
  • Hotdog Buns: like the hotdogs any pre-packaged hotdog buns will do
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Optional ingredients:

  • Relish
  • Onions
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Throw in microwave after wrapping in paper towel and cook that bad boy for about <=1minute 30 seconds (This time depends on your microwave and could vary +or- 30seconds. The idea is to get it blistered / imploded just a tad bit).
  2. Put the hotdog on the hotdog bun.
  3. Add condiements.
